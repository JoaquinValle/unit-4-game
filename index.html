Crystals Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystal colors below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching yout total score to the random number You lose the gmae if your total score goes over the number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts the game will change the values of each crystal.
